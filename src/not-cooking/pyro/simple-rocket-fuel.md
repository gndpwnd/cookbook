# Simple Rocket Fuel

## Fuel Formulas
**Medium burn rate**
* 61gm KNO3
* 4gm NH4NO3
* 35gm sorbitol
* 1cc glycerine

**Slow burn rate**
* 46gm KNO3
* 15gm NH4ClO4
* 4gm NH4NO3
* 35gm sorbitol
* 1cc glycerine

**Fast burn rate**
* 61gm KNO3
* 4gm NH4NO3
* 35gm sorbitol
* 1 gm ferric oxide (red)
* 1cc glycerine
