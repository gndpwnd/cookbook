# Protein Shakes

**Ingredients**

* 1C Milk
* 1/2C Yogurt (Ex: 1 Tilamook Container)
* 2 Scoops Chocolate Protein Powder
* 1T Cocoa Powder
* 1T Peanut Butter
* 1 Banana

**Optional Ingredients**

* 1C Spinach
* 1/3C pomegranate juice
* 2T Ground Flax



**Directions**

Combine in a blender.
